\## \*\*What Successful Cabo Buyers Actually Do Differently\*\*



I've noticed something interesting about the buyers who actually pull the trigger and end up happy with their purchase.



\*\*They don't start by looking at properties.\*\*



They start by answering five essential questions that most people never even think to ask. These questions create a filter that turns the chaos of "looking at everything" into the clarity of "knowing exactly what I want and why."



Here's what I mean...



\### \*\*Question 1: What's your true Los Cabos lifestyle vision?\*\*

Not "I want a nice place by the beach." But really - \*\*how do you actually want to spend your time here?\*\* Are you the type who wants to walk to restaurants, or do you prefer total privacy? Do you want to be where the action is, or do you want your own slice of paradise?



\### \*\*Question 2: What's your complete investment budget?\*\*

Not just the purchase price. \*\*The real number that includes closing costs, furnishing, ongoing expenses, everything.\*\* Because getting surprised by an extra $50K in costs you didn't know about isn't fun for anyone.



\### \*\*Question 3: Which location actually matches your goals?\*\*

Los Cabos isn't just "Los Cabos." Palmilla feels completely different from downtown Cabo. Pedregal has a different vibe than the Corridor. \*\*Most people pick a location based on one pretty photo instead of understanding what each area is actually like.\*\*



\### \*\*Question 4: What's the primary purpose of this property?\*\*

Personal use? Investment? A bit of both? \*\*This changes everything about what you should buy and where.\*\* But most people try to optimize for everything and end up with something that doesn't really work for anything.



\### \*\*Question 5: Are you actually ready to move forward?\*\*

Not "someday when the market is perfect." But \*\*are you ready to make a decision in the next few months if we find the right property?\*\* Because if you're not, that's fine - but it changes how we should approach this whole thing.
